Knowledge Bar

Phishing

Fraudulent emails pretending to be from trusted sources to steal sensitive data.

Smishing

Phishing attacks carried out via SMS or text messages.

Vishing

Voice-based scams where attackers impersonate legitimate organizations over phone calls.

Pharming

Redirecting users from legitimate websites to fake ones without their knowledge.

Spear Phishing

Highly targeted phishing attacks aimed at specific individuals or organizations.
